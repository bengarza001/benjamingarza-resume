Benjamin Garza

CEO

I have always been fascinated by the study of history. The study of different cultures, the evolved, and how politics shapes our lives ... I have found that history books teach us not only how to learn from past mistakes but more importantly, reveal truths about ourselves. This fascination with history has inspired me to go beyond history textbooks to gain first hand knowledge about how the real world works. Since High School, I have volunteered my time working in a political office. Working with local elected officials to serve the community I reside in not only exposed me to the many issues affecting my neighborhood but more importantly, it afforded me the opportunity to help needy residents. For example, I listened to constituents about the issues most important to them and assured them their opinion mattered. I was able to directly speak to politicians and advocate on behalf of the neediest of residents in my community.

Beyond the classroom, I have prepared myself by examining other potential interests as I was a part of the Nasa Community College Aerospace Scholar(NCAS). This highly sought after program is typically reserved for only STEM majors or those seeking a career in STEM, however I was fortunate enough to be accepted into that course. In this 5 week course I learned about NASA and its missions. To reinforce learning I took quizzes based on the missions which eventually led up to one giant research project. Not only did I learn a lot about NASA and the work they do in helping the world but I learned the skills required in a high-paced environment where work must be done over a set period of time in order to ensure total accuracy of data.

These experiences have helped shape my life and passions and have prepared me for my intended major. The time I have spent dedicating my life to public service and helping those in need, within the classroom learning about political science and the journey I took through the NASA Aerospace Scholar program has prepared me to learn so much more to catapult me into a successful career and future.
